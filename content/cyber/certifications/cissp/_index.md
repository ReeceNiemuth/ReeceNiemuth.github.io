The CISSP (Certified Information Systems Security Professional) is a globally recognized certification for information security professionals.

---

## Exam Overview

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Domains** | 8 |
| **Questions** | 125-175 (CAT format) |
| **Duration** | 4 hours |
| **Passing Score** | 700/1000 |
| **Experience Required** | 5 years in 2+ domains |

---

## The 8 Domains

1. **Security and Risk Management** (15%)
2. **Asset Security** (10%)
3. **Security Architecture and Engineering** (13%)
4. **Communication and Network Security** (13%)
5. **Identity and Access Management (IAM)** (13%)
6. **Security Assessment and Testing** (12%)
7. **Security Operations** (13%)
8. **Software Development Security** (11%)

## My Tips

1. **Think like a manager**, not a technician
2. Focus on **risk management** - it's woven throughout
3. Use the **MindMap videos** for big-picture understanding
4. Take practice exams to **identify weak areas**
5. Don't memorize - **understand the concepts**
