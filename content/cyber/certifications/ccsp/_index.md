The CCSP (Certified Cloud Security Professional) demonstrates expertise in cloud security architecture, design, operations, and service orchestration.

---

## Exam Overview

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Domains** | 6 |
| **Questions** | 125 |
| **Duration** | 3 hours |
| **Passing Score** | 700/1000 |
| **Experience Required** | 5 years IT (3 in security, 1 in cloud) |

---

## The 6 Domains

1. **Cloud Concepts, Architecture and Design** (17%)
2. **Cloud Data Security** (20%)
3. **Cloud Platform & Infrastructure Security** (17%)
4. **Cloud Application Security** (17%)
5. **Cloud Security Operations** (16%)
6. **Legal, Risk and Compliance** (13%)

## CISSP vs CCSP

If you have CISSP, CCSP builds on that foundation with cloud-specific knowledge. Many concepts overlap, especially in governance, risk, and compliance.
