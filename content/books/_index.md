## Cybersecurity & Technology

| Book | Author | Why I Recommend It |
|------|--------|-------------------|
| *The Phoenix Project* | Gene Kim | Essential DevOps/IT operations novel |
| *Sandworm* | Andy Greenberg | Gripping account of nation-state cyber warfare |
| *Countdown to Zero Day* | Kim Zetter | The definitive Stuxnet story |
| *This Is How They Tell Me the World Ends* | Nicole Perlroth | Eye-opening look at the zero-day market |
| *Practical Malware Analysis* | Sikorski & Honig | Hands-on reverse engineering guide |
